Enter the Ring!

Welcome to the fourth year of Sunshine CTF, hosted by Hack@UCF (the Collegiate Cyber Defense Club @ UCF) in affiliation with B-Sides Orlando 2019. We've created challenges around this year's theme: Wrestling. No folding chairs or cages necessary!

Remote participants are allowed and encouraged, however the winning team must be present at B-Sides Orlando 2019 to be presented with a trophy at closing ceremonies. This means that the trophy will be given to the in-person competitor who is in first place at 4 PM on Saturday, not necessarily to the person who wins the competition overall.
