Welcome to the fifth year of Sunshine CTF, hosted by Hack@UCF (the Collegiate Cyber Defense Club @ UCF) in affiliation with B-Sides Orlando 2020. We've created challenges around this year's theme: Floridian Vacations. Prepare your mouse ears and beach chairs!

Remote participants are allowed and encouraged, however the winning team must be present at B-Sides Orlando 2020 to be presented with a trophy at closing ceremonies. This means that the trophy will be given to the in-person competitor who is in first place at 4 PM on Saturday, not necessarily to the person who wins the competition overall.
